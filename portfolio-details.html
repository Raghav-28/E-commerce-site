Black Rudraksh Mala

The Black Rudraksha Mala is a sacred rosary made from black rudraksha beads, renowned for their spiritual significance and healing properties. Each bead typically measures around 7-8 mm in diameter, strung together to form a traditional mala with 108 beads plus one guru bead.

Size: Beads are approximately 7-8 mm in diameter.

Benefits:

Spiritual Protection: Black rudraksha beads are believed to offer protection from negative energies and enhance spiritual well-being.

Meditative Aid: Using the mala during meditation helps to calm the mind, improve focus, and deepen spiritual practices.

Healing Properties: It is said that wearing or chanting with the mala can balance energy levels, promote physical healing, and reduce stress.

Connection to Lord Shiva: Considered sacred in Hinduism, the mala is often associated with devotion to Lord Shiva and aids in cultivating a deeper connection to spiritual realms.

The Black Rudraksha Mala serves as both a spiritual tool and a wearable accessory, cherished for its profound benefits and cultural significance in various spiritual practices.
